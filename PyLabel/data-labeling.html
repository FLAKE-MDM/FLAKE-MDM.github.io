DATA labeling
All types of data labeling:
Images

From small details of the face to pictures from space

Over the years , we have placed more than 100,000,000 images. Let's analyze emotions, select the necessary objects, assign labels and comments.

Videos

Detection of faces in a crowd and any objects in motion

We will find an object in motion and track its path, build a motion forecast, and classify it by any parameters.

Audio

Bots, voice assistants and phonetics

We will arrange accents and punctuation, decipher children's speech and bad pronunciation, and transcribe audio recordings.

Lidar Data

Abstract of 3D models, maps and the environment

Let's define the boundaries of the object in space, track its track and build a forecast of movement using cuboids and tags.

Text

Computer and handwritten text in 30+ languages

We recognize the language and translate the text for processing, assemble, structure and classify the text for the task.

Healthcare Data

Marking of all types of medical data from dental crowns to MRI images

We cooperate with doctors, dentists and federal health centers in many countries of the world.

stages of work
How it usually works

1. Application and free pilot

After your application, we will contact you. You will receive a test dataset or a sample markup. We coordinate the quality, determine the controversial points, calculate the speed and cost of markup.

2. Conclusion of the contract

We legally guarantee the quality at the highest level. This is a typical contract, but if necessary, we can modify it to suit your compliance.

3. Team assignment

We appoint an experienced project manager and assemble a team of taggers. For industry projects, we have medical workers, linguists, biologists, engineers in our team.

4. Progress of work

Daily, weekly, monthly reporting and 24/7 project management consultation.

5. Verification and payment

Pay only after receiving 100% of the markup quality, which was agreed upon at the pilot.
